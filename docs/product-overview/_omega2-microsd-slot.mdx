The MicroSD card slot can be used to extend the available storage. 

#### To connect a MicroSD card

1. Insert the MicroSD card into the slot
1. Push it in to secure it until you hear a click
1. The Linux operating system will automatically mount the filesystem.

#### To remove the MicroSD card

1. Unmount the SD cardfilesystem in Linux
1. Press down on the card until you hear a click
1. Release, and the card will eject.

For more information on using the MicroSD card slot, see the [SDIO article](/hardware-interfaces/sdio).
